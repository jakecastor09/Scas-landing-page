HISTORY OF ST. CHARLES AUGUSTINE SCHOOL OF BIÑAN LAGUNA INC.

When we saw that there was a felt need to put up a Preparatory school in the community, we inspired to start a small school by using a small lot (garage) as the site of this learning center. Establishing a school was not so easy as there are lot of factors to be considered. For us, little kids who got to school in their early ages should have the notion that schooling is a worthwhile and happy experience and not a boring plain learning alone. Thus, the very infant Kindergarten and Nursery was named “Little Wonders Learning Center”. It is a school that communes with the community and it is a learning place where neighboring mothers and grandmothers don’t have to suffer a long way to travel to send their children and grandchildren to school away from busy streets hazards and from violent laden society in other words, accessibility to the school is favorable to the students...

S.C.A.S commits itself: in tapping and developing the child’s inherent talents and skills, in expanding his horizon, and in providing him the highest standard of education at a price affordable even to the below average-income families.

S.C.A.S believes that a child who is closely supervised and exposed to a stimulating learning environment, resources and approaches is enabled to learn develop his multiple intelligences and abilities and is encouraged to socialize with his peers gaining an optimum chance of attaining his academic goals and improving his personal relationship.
